Research Paper
Geopolitics · AI Governance
Algorithmic Colonization: How AI & Data Infrastructure Are Reshaping Global Power Dynamics

Conducted research on the evolving role of algorithms as instruments of geopolitical power, introducing the concept of algorithmic colonization — how control over data, AI systems, and computational infrastructure reshapes global power dynamics. The study proposes an Algorithmic Geopolitical Hierarchy classifying nations as algorithmic producers, consumers, or dependents, and examines Big Tech as quasi-sovereign entities. Analyzing AI governance models across the U.S., EU, and China, the research argues that future geopolitical conflicts will center on digital infrastructure rather than physical borders.
